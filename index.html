A Hopeless Romantic

You have always been close. Only one chance encounter away. Each day I feel closer, yet, you make me endure. You come into my life - then leave just as quickly; leaving me with the sour aftertaste. One moment my everything, the next a memory. Returning to your constant state of being a stranger, only present for an intimate instant. The greatest science unable to rekindle the spark which only coincidence could construct.

I forever resided in a state of dreaming paralysis; consuming my mind daily with eternal infatuation. Yet I wonder if it heightens my bliss, my future euphoria. Or simply diminishes my reality, rendering the future to prove insufficient. You associate yourself with insecurity, connotate with suffering. Is this your revenge? A trade for all you bring. Love. A word feeling insufficient, 4 letters to represent a lifetime of emotions.
